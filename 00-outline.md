## How to Use This Manual

**For new engagements:**
Start with Chapter 1 to define scope, proceed through Chapters 2-6 for discovery and analysis, then Chapters 7-8 for assessment and reporting.

**For updates to existing threat models:**
Focus on Chapter 9 for maintenance procedures, revisit relevant discovery chapters (2-5) as needed.

**For specific methodologies:**
Jump to Chapter 6 for detailed framework guidance.

**For reporting:**
Use Chapter 8 templates and Appendix materials.
